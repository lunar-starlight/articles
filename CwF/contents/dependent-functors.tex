\section{Dependent presehaves}

\begin{definition}
  A \emph{dependent functor \(Q\)} over a functor \(P\) is a functor of type
  \(\int P → \cat D\). We denote it by \(Q : P → \cat D\).

  A \emph{dependent presheaf} is then a contravariant dependent functor with
  codomain \(\sets\).
\end{definition}
